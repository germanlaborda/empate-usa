Who we are

An international model, now in the United States

Empate is a nonprofit organization founded in Argentina that creates free sports, cultural and development programs for individuals with Down syndrome.

Over the past nine years, Empate has expanded across Argentina and Peru, building inclusive community-based programs with proven impact.

Empate USA represents the next step: bringing this validated methodology to the United States.

First program

Launching with our core program

Empate USA will begin with a free inclusive football school open to individuals with Down syndrome of all ages.

  • Physical development
  • Social inclusion
  • Confidence and autonomy
  • Family engagement
  • Volunteer participation

Program format: 2 sessions/week • 90 minutes • 10-month cycle

Why it matters

Why Empate USA

Despite growing awareness around disability inclusion in the United States, access to structured, high-quality and free development programs for individuals with Down syndrome remains limited — particularly within multicultural communities.

South Florida offers a strategic entry point due to its diverse population, strong sports culture and international visibility.

Long-term vision

Building more than a program

  • A leading inclusion platform in South Florida
  • A community hub for families and volunteers
  • A trusted institutional partner
  • A long-term fundraising bridge supporting Empate programs across Latin America
